Who am I

About me

I am an Argentinian graphic designer graduated from the University of Buenos Aires (U.B.A). I specialized in product design, advertisements and interface design. For years I was the development director of my own company, Estudio Saltamonte, in the city of Buenos Aires.

Professional development

During that time I gave lectures at several universities in Argentina, such as the U.P. (University of Palermo) and the U.B.A. in this one I became a professor for two years in the chair of D.G.P.C. (Graphic design in website interfaces).

Thanks to my work for renowned Argentine artists as Caloi and Liniers and companies such as Microsoft and Disney, my design studio won the award for the best website in all of Argentina (MATE.AR) for two consecutive years, and the “Site of the day” in the FWA, which later was features in the book ACA PONER EL LIBRO

Ten years later, I left the studio to create a new company for the develop and production of design objects. For this reason, I entered the world of coding, acquiring knowledge in languages such as C, C# and Php that allowed me to have a complete overview of the development and design of websites, and desktop apps.

Future

I love to be updated with the latest technologies and trends in my profession, since I consider that one of the best qualities that one can have is curiosity and the desire for constant improvement.
